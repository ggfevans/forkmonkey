## Tips for LinkedIn

1. **Post timing**: Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10am local time
2. **Engage**: Reply to every comment within 2 hours
3. **No links in post**: Put link in first comment or use "link in bio"
4. **Use emojis**: But not too many
5. **Tag people**: If someone helped, tag them
6. **Hashtags**: 3-5 relevant ones at the end
